about

Welcome to Rockland Fitness Academy!

The coaching staff is looking forward to helping you reach your fitness goals. It is also important that all potential clients know we value your well being and all our programming will follow our motto... Fitness is rewarding not punishment!

At our core is the Triple H Principle:

  • Head: We educate each client;
  • Heart: It is important that each session has its purpose and direction; and
  • Hand: Each client needs to know that they have to put the work in every training session.

The is the Kid Fit Program that addresses general fitness for your youngster at our studio or at the school. We have Evolution Training Systems, which is our sports performance training for athletes at all levels. We offer training in either private, semi-private, small groups or team sessions. There is also Fitness Bootcamp 4 Life which is the adult fitness program offered three times a day, everyday!

kid fit

After school fitness program, "Kid Fit", is for youth between the ages 9 and 13. We focus on the fundamentals of fitness that can be applied to life, school and sports. In this age of specialization, a lot of our youth lack the basic understanding and ability when it comes to strength and body control. Our smal class format will work on body weight strength exercises, agility work and flexibility training. There will also be fitness games to work on dynamic movement patterns and problem solving.

sports performance training

Our program, "Evolution Training Systems" is designed to help all athletes reach their full potential. This is for athletes who have shown the ability to perform all the basic fitness fundamentals. In this next level training, the athletes will focus on building strength and power through progressive weight training. The goal is to maximize your genetic potential through hard work, proper form and superior focus.
