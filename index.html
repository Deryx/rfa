about

Welcome to Rockland Fitness Academy!

The coaching staff is looking forward to helping you reach your fitness goals. It is also important that all potential clients know we value your well being and all our programming will follow our motto... Fitness is rewarding not punishment!

At our core is the Triple H Principle:

  • Head: We educate each client;
  • Heart: It is important that each session has its purpose and direction; and
  • Hand: Each client needs to know that they have to put the work in every training session.

The is the Kid Fit Program that addresses general fitness for your youngster at our studio or at the school. We have Evolution Training Systems, which is our sports performance training for athletes at all levels. We offer training in either private, semi-private, small groups or team sessions. There is also Fitness Bootcamp 4 Life which is the adult fitness program offered three times a day, everyday!
